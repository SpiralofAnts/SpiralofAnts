Hey, heads up! Please be patient with the buttons. The way things are set up, when you click a button, it stops doing anything else until the song loads. That means if you click a button multiple times, it'll load the same audio multiple times and it'll sound weird, so please be patient!!

To give a little more context, every piece of music on this page loops infinitely and seamlessly once begun, and it can be stopped and restarted at any time.

Additionally, only one song can be played at a time. I know this doesn't sound like a whole lot, but it took me roughly six hours just to get a skeleton of this working, and an additional three just to figure out how to get other songs to stop when you start one.

In any case though, this is just a proof of concept. I plan to make a few pages with much more songs so that the public might enjoy looped music, handily accessible from wherever they might be.
